Ticket Categorization & Routing

This project aims to implement automated ticket categorization and routing rules within Service Desk Plus, enhancing response times, prioritization, and overall service desk efficiency.

Project Overview
Title Ticket Categorization & Routing
Description Implement automated processes to categorize incoming service tickets and route them to the appropriate support team, reducing manual intervention and enhancing efficiency.
Objective To improve service desk response times, ensure faster resolution, and optimize ticket handling by implementing automated categorization and routing mechanisms.
Scope The project includes setting up automated categorization rules, routing logic for different ticket types, training service desk agents, and monitoring the process for continuous improvement.
Stakeholders IT support staff, service desk managers, end-users, and department heads.
Business Case
Business Need To reduce ticket handling times, increase service desk productivity, and ensure that tickets are assigned to the right support teams quickly and accurately.
Benefits
  • Improved ticket response and resolution times.
  • Reduced workload for service desk agents through automation.
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction due to faster service delivery.
  • Better resource allocation and workload management.
ROI Increased efficiency, reduced manual errors, improved service delivery, and more streamlined support operations.
Project Deliverables
Deliverables
  • Configured ticket categorization rules in Service Desk Plus.
  • Automated routing rules for different support teams.
  • Training materials and sessions for service desk staff.
  • Documentation of automated processes and routing logic.
Success Criteria Successful reduction of average ticket resolution times by 30% and increased accuracy of ticket routing by over 90%.
Implementation Plan
Methodology The implementation will follow an Agile approach, beginning with a pilot setup, feedback collection, iterative adjustments, and full rollout across the service desk.
Tools & Technologies Service Desk Plus, automated workflows, categorization and routing algorithms, and ITSM integration tools.
Governance & Compliance
Governance Structure Managed by the IT service management team, with oversight from service desk managers to ensure compliance with internal service standards and data handling policies.
Compliance Adherence to ITIL principles, data protection regulations, and internal service desk policies.
Documentation
Technical Documentation Detailed guides for configuring categorization rules, automated routing setups, and integration with Service Desk Plus.
User Documentation User manuals, process flow diagrams, and training videos for service desk agents to understand the automated routing process.
Project Closure
Final Review Conduct a final review with service desk managers to ensure the automated categorization and routing are functioning as expected, with adjustments made based on feedback.
Handover Complete handover to the service desk management team, including training, user documentation, and ongoing support plans.
Sign-Off Obtain final sign-off from IT service management and service desk managers, confirming successful deployment and operation of the automated ticket categorization and routing system.

SMS School

Mastering Service Desk Plus

Welcome to the Service Management and Support (SMS) School, where learners dive into the essentials of IT service management using Service Desk Plus. This program prepares individuals to excel in managing service requests, incidents, and changes within an IT service delivery framework. Through hands-on training, we cover ITIL best practices, automation, and optimizing workflows to enhance IT support capabilities in modern organizations.

Overview of Service Desk Plus Capabilities

The SMS School offers comprehensive training in Service Desk Plus, covering incident management, problem resolution, and service request fulfillment. Learners will gain hands-on experience in configuring, managing, and optimizing IT support operations.

Incident Management

Learn how to manage and resolve incidents efficiently, minimizing downtime and maintaining high levels of service availability.

Change Management

Implement effective change management processes that ensure smooth transitions while minimizing risks and service disruptions.

Automated Workflows

Configure and deploy automated workflows to streamline service desk operations, reduce manual tasks, and enhance productivity.

Hands-On Learning Labs

The SMS School offers practical labs that allow learners to apply IT service management concepts in simulated environments. These labs prepare learners to manage IT services in real-world scenarios, focusing on problem-solving and process optimization.

Configuring Service Desk Modules

Set up and manage core modules in Service Desk Plus, including Incident, Change, and Problem Management, to deliver top-tier IT support services.

Implementing ITIL Workflows

Build and configure ITIL-aligned workflows within Service Desk Plus, enabling learners to implement best practices for IT service management.

Real-World Projects in Service Delivery

The SMS School includes projects that replicate the service management challenges faced by enterprises, preparing learners to handle diverse IT support needs with confidence.

Deploying a Self-Service Portal

Design and implement a self-service portal to empower users with knowledge base articles, automated ticket generation, and FAQ resources.

Building a Custom Dashboard

Create a custom dashboard for IT managers, offering insights into ticket volumes, response times, SLA compliance, and service metrics.

Certification & Career Advancement

The SMS School prepares learners for IT service management roles, focusing on certifications like ITIL and Service Desk Plus, paving the way for successful careers in IT support and service delivery.

ITIL Foundation Certification

Learn the core concepts of ITIL, covering service strategy, design, transition, operation, and continual service improvement, preparing learners for the ITIL Foundation exam.

Service Desk Plus Certified Professional

Master the tools and techniques in Service Desk Plus, preparing learners for the official certification and real-world IT service management roles.

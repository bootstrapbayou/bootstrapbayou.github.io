Welcome to SMS School Labs

Dive into hands-on labs centered on Service Desk Plus, focusing on IT service management, automation, and user support. Labs are designed to build your skills in service delivery and optimization, helping you manage real-world challenges in ITSM.

Lab Categories

Explore lab categories focused on ticketing management, IT asset management, process automation, and reporting within Service Desk Plus. Each category offers practical labs that enhance your IT service management skills.

Ticketing Management

Learn to manage incidents, service requests, and escalations efficiently using Service Desk Plus.

IT Asset Management
